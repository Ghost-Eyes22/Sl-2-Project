Spiderman Is

Spiderman represents a set of ideals that anyone can aspire to. The character isnt perfect, but he does try every day to make the world a better place and to better himself. Spider-Man takes his responsibility seriously, while looking for ways to keep his friends, family, and even strangers safe. Spider-Mans core message is With great power comes great responsibility. The proverb is everywhere and popularized by Spider-Man in Marvel films,comics, and other media.
